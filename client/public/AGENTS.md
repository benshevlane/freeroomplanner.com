# AGENTS.md — Free Room Planner

Free Room Planner (https://freeroomplanner.com) is a free, browser-based room and floor
planning tool for homeowners. Users draw a room to scale, drag in furniture and
fixtures, and export the plan to PDF. No sign-up, download, or payment required.

## Key pages
- `/` — the planner app (draw your floor plan)
- `/room-planner`, `/kitchen-planner`, `/bathroom-planner`, `/bedroom-planner`,
  `/living-room-planner` — room-specific planning tools
- `/floor-plan-maker` — full floor plan maker
- `/how-it-works` — how the tool works
- `/blog` — room planning guides and tutorials
- `/get-embed` — embed the planner on another site

## Conventions
- All measurements are drawn to scale; users set room dimensions in millimetres.
- Content is UK-oriented (fixtures use standard UK sizes).
- The tool runs entirely client-side; no account is needed to plan or export.
