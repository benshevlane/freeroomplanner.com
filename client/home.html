How it works

Blank page to shared plan in three steps

No training required. If you can drag a file, you can draw a floor plan.

1

Draw your walls

Click to drop wall points. They snap to 90° and a 1 cm grid, with live measurements as you go — straight lines without measuring twice.

2

Add furniture

Drag sofas, beds, tables, doors and windows from the library. Resize and rotate to fit. The canvas pans as you draw, so you never run out of room.

3

Save & share

Hit Save to get a shareable link and download a clean PNG for your kitchen maker, fitter or builder. Your plan saves automatically, so you can reopen and tweak it anytime.
